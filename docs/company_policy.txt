Company Policy Document

## Employee Handbook

### Working Hours
Our standard working hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Flexible work arrangements are available upon approval from your direct supervisor.

### Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. All remote work must be pre-approved and documented in our HR system.

### Vacation Policy
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

### Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave can be used for personal illness or to care for immediate family members.

### Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct. This includes:
- Treating colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintaining confidentiality of company information
- Following all applicable laws and regulations
- Reporting any unethical behavior to HR

### IT Security
- All company devices must have up-to-date antivirus software
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- No personal software may be installed on company devices
- All data must be backed up to company servers

### Performance Reviews
Annual performance reviews are conducted in December. Goals and objectives are set at the beginning of each year and reviewed quarterly.

### Benefits
- Health insurance coverage for employees and dependents
- 401(k) matching up to 6% of salary
- Life insurance coverage equal to annual salary
- Professional development budget of $2,000 per year
